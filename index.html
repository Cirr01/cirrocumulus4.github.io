How to use this website

Open the file in your browser.
Go to TPS Data Entry and type room counts, shifts, and current chair inventory.
Check MIS Reports for room capacity, simultaneous demand, and chair shortages.
Use DSS Forecast to show future chair needs during your presentation.
Click Export CSV if you want a copy of your browser data.

Important notes

The site starts with Legazpi City schools from your uploaded workbook.
Most actual planning fields are blank on purpose, because those are the real values you still need to request.
The Load Demo Inputs button generates sample values for testing only.
All changes are stored locally in your own browser until you reset them.
